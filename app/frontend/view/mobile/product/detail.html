Rechargeable Battery Operated Fan With 3 Speeds

A life saver on hot days

3 Speeds Cool You Down Fast

The ingenious fan features three speeds: low, medium, and high, fit-ting anyone's cooling needs. Just press the button to switch the fan on and adjust wind modes.

A Portable But Furthermore, A Desktop Fan

It can be a handheld fan or as a desktop air fan according to your individual needs. With a plus stand you can use it as a desktop fan as well.

Dual Power Source

This fan comes with a built-in 1200mAh 18650 Li-ion battery, working up to 8 hours after a full charge. It can also be used via USB port from your computer, power bank, USB charger or other devices with USB output.

Choose Your Desire Direction of Wind

The head of this fan can rotates up to 180 degrees and the handle is extendable. Therefore, so you can direct the cool air to where you need it most.

Enjoy A Whispering Breeze

The fan moves loads of air yet it is very quiet. Its brushless motor keeps you cool and comfortable while you are concentrating or sleeping.

Removable Grill Cool and Clean

Fans would accumulate dirt and dust overtime. While the grill of our fan can be removed easily by turning it anticlockwise. Place the grill in a sink and turn on the faucet. Dust the blades with a brush or sponge.
